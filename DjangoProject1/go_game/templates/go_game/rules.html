How to Play Go

Objective

Surround more territory than your opponent by placing stones on the board.

Placing Stones

Players alternate placing black and white stones on intersections.

Capturing Stones

If a stone or group has no liberties (empty adjacent intersections), it is captured and removed.

Suicide Rule

You cannot place a stone that has no liberties unless it captures opponent stones.

Ko Rule

You cannot immediately repeat the previous board position.
